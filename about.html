About Me

Educator | Innovator | Mentor

My Story


Prince Oduro is an educator, mentor, and technology leader committed to empowering young Africans through innovation, creativity, and digital education. As the founder of GLAD Innovation Hub, he leads initiatives that develop practical digital skills and foster a culture of problem-solving and innovation among young people.

With a strong background in technology and years of experience in teaching and mentorship, his work is centered on helping individuals unlock their potential in an evolving digital world. He designs and implements hands-on training programs and builds innovation networks that connect learning with real-world impact across Africa.

Prince also serves as Program Coordinator at Dodoo Coding Club in Ghana, where he drives initiatives that nurture the next generation of tech innovators and digital entrepreneurs. His leadership promotes inclusive, experiential learning environments that encourage creativity, collaboration, and leadership in technology.

Guided by a clear vision, Prince is dedicated to expanding access to technology education across Africa and building a generation of confident, innovative thinkers prepared to lead in a technology-driven future.
