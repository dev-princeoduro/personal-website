About Me

Educator | Innovator | Mentor

My Story


Prince Oduro is an educator, mentor, and technology leader dedicated to empowering young Africans through innovation, creativity, and digital education. With a strong background in software development and years of experience in teaching and mentorship, his work focuses on helping others discover and develop their potential in the fast-changing world of technology.

Prince leads initiatives that equip young people with hands-on tech skills while building smart digital solutions for businesses and communities. At the Hub, he oversees strategy, designs practical training programs, and builds campus-based innovation networks that connect learning with real-world impact across Africa.

In his role as Program Coordinator at Dodoo Coding Club in Ghana, Prince drives programs that nurture the next generation of tech innovators and digital entrepreneurs. His leadership promotes inclusive, experiential learning environments where young minds are inspired to create, collaborate, and lead confidently in technology.

With a clear vision for the future, Prince is committed to expanding access to tech education across Africa, cultivating a culture of innovation, critical thinking, and continuous learning that empowers young people to become leaders and changemakers in a technology-driven world.
